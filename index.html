Mary Elis Tharin

Mary Elis is a writer of speculative and contemporary fiction living in Treviso, Italy.

Her work has appeared in Sixfold, Five on the Fifth, NER's Behind the Byline, and elsewhere. She is currently pursuing an MFA in creative writing at Antioch University, and is an editor on Lunch Ticket.

Find her on instagram @elis_tharin or contact her at hello@marytharin.com.
